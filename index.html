UOSC

The ultimate old school console

View project onGitHub

Think of this as a launcher, it's supposed to give you a "Big Picture"-like mode for enjoying your favorite old school games.

Requirements

Of course this project has some requirements...

  • SDL 2.0
  • SDL2_Image 2.0
  • SDL2_TTF 2.0
  • yaml-cpp
  • Boost Filesystem
